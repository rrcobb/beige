This collection brings together the minimal, thoughtful websites that have emerged around AI predictions and timeline discussions. These sites share a common aesthetic—clean typography, muted colors, and a focus on substantive content over flashy design.

The "beige prediction sites" represent a particular genre of AI commentary: detailed, technical analyses presented in deliberately understated visual formats. They tend to focus on concrete timelines, specific scenarios, and quantified risk assessments rather than abstract speculation.

The collections and discussions section includes the broader ecosystem of researchers, writers, and communities engaged in serious AI safety and capability discussions. These range from academic research organizations to individual bloggers who have built influential voices in the space.

Together, these resources represent much of the current landscape of thoughtful, technical AI prediction and analysis—presented in the same minimalist spirit as the sites themselves.
